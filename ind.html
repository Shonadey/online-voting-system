INSTRUCTIONS FOR USERS TO CAST THEIR VOTES SYSTEMATICALLY

Registering to Vote: Click on the "Register Now To Vote" link in the "Elector Services" section. Fill out the required information accurately, including your personal details and address. Verify your identity and eligibility according to the guidelines provided. Upon successful registration, you will receive a confirmation message with further instructions. Checking Application Status: Navigate to the "Check Application Status" link under the "Elector Services" section. Enter your application reference number or any other required information. Click on the "Check Status" button to view the current status of your application. If your application is pending, processing, or approved, you will receive relevant updates. Locating Your Polling Booth: Access the "Know Your Polling Booth" link in the "Elector Services" section. Enter your address or location details, including city, state, and zip code. Click on the "Find Polling Booth" button to retrieve information about your designated polling station. Make note of the polling booth address and any other relevant details for future reference. Searching Name in Voter List: Select the "Search Name in Voter List" option from the "Elector Services" section. Enter your details such as name, date of birth, and address as per the registration records. Click on the "Search" button to verify if your name is included in the voter list. If your name is found, you are eligible to vote in the upcoming elections. Registering Complaints: If you encounter any issues or have concerns related to the electoral process, use the "Register Complaint" link. Provide a detailed description of the complaint, including relevant information such as date, time, and location. Submit your complaint through the online form and await further communication from the electoral authorities. Rest assured that your complaint will be addressed promptly and appropriately by the designated officials.
